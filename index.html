Nova's Adventurers

The Nova Sphere

"This is the name of the setting of my friend group's Dungeons & Dragons campaign, created by our Dungeon Master. As a Spelljammer campaign, it takes place in a space-like environment with five planets, which are all enveloped within a barrier known as a crystal sphere. The planets are: Carthon, a world taken over by technology; Shardbor, a rocky planet covered in wastelands; Hydronis, the Water Planet; Frithmoor, a planet with vast forests; and Nelvon, where a harsh tundra encompasses all. There's also Mountaincall, a mountain range floating in the middle of space after being somehow removed from Carthon. These worlds give rise to a variety of folks."

Monty, Aquisition & Renovation

"An aetherborn gunslinger under the employ of the ruling family of Mountaincall, Monty hails from a different crystal sphere entirely, and seems to have brought some baggage with him. He was sent to track a mysterious ship that dropped a payload onto the floating city. The journey has led him to pick up a mismatched group of comrades."

Azalea

"An enigmatic woman, Azalea is a recent addition to the crew. She posesses considerable martial skill, as well as strange magical abilities."

Mort U. Arry

"Another newcomer, Mort is much like any other dwarf you'd find: fond of alcohol and working hard in order to play hard later. What sets him apart is his skill in firing his Flametounge Bow and affinity for nature magic."

Paelen Brielle

"Paelen used to work alongside Lucille and Valindra--the current captain of the ship the party is using--as a mercenary. The elf has an affinity for healing and lightning magic, as well as a rather unusual pink coloration."

Lucille Belle

"Last, but certainly not least, is the character I play. Lucy is a half-vampire who was living among the people of Carthon, until the front of the herbalism shop run by her and Paelen was blown up by a conflict that Monty had found himself in. Her normaly inteligent, if somewhat melancholy, demeanor changes dramatically in battle to resemble that of a wild beast."
